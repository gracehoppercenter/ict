Welcome to the Grace Hopper Center's Computer Science and Web Development portal!

We have an integrated curriculum that will provide students with a deep understanding of what a computer is and what it can do, while also allowing students to leave us with job ready skills as entry level web developers.

You are invited to browse this site to see examples of student work and projects, and the class materials and assignments we use here in our CS and ICT classes. So enjoy your visit and come back often. There are exciting things going on at the Grace Hopper Center!
